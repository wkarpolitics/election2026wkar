Our Promise

What voters get from us.

Eight counties. Capitol to Main Street. This is our coverage.

  • In-Depth Reporting

    No 10-second soundbites. Our hosts ask the hard questions that matter to daily life and give candidates and officials the time to answer.

  • Beyond The Capitol

    We go to the small towns and outlying counties where the stories are just as consequential, just as personal, and far less told.

  • Reporting Beyond The Obvious

    We find the overlooked angles, the undercovered communities, and the decisions that shape your life close to home.

  • Tools To Take Action

    Coverage is paired with resources to understand your ballot, contact representatives, and make your voice heard locally and statewide.

  • Where You Are

    A new video podcast studio will bring more of our team to the platforms where you already spend time: more voices, more formats, more access.

  • Independent. Always.

    WKAR is public media: no advertisers, no corporate owners, no political agenda. Independent journalists work for you, and only you.
